Frequently Asked Questions

What is the TRM Community Game Awards?

The TRM Community Game Awards is a game award show presented by Michael Webb, aka “TheRandomMelon”, on his Twitch channel. The goal of the show is to allow the audience to truly run the show by allowing winners to be chosen by the audience (from a select number of candidates decided by the TRM Community Game Awards Nomination Committee).

Who is the Nomination Committee?

The Nomination Committee is a group hand-picked by show creator Michael Webb that decides what categories will exist, along with what nominees will exist for each category. See the about page for more info on the members.

When can I vote?

See the voting page for the latest on voting.

When is the award ceremony/where is the ceremony hosted?

The TRM Community Game Awards 2024 will be broadcasted live on Twitch on December 30, 2024, starting at 6 PM CST. Watch live at twitch.tv/therandommelon.

Is there a Discord server?

Yes! Participate with the community and get announcements first on the TRM Community Discord server.
