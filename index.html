About The Show

The TRM Community Game Awards 2024 is a game award show presented by Michael Webb, aka “TheRandomMelon”, on his Twitch channel, twitch.tv/therandommelon. The goal of the show is to allow the audience to truly run the show by allowing winners to be chosen by the audience (from a select number of candidates decided by the TRM Community Game Awards Nomination Committee).

Participate with the community and get announcements first on the TRM Community Discord server!
